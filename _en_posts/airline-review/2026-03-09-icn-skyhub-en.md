### Incheon Airport Terminal 2 SkyHub Lounge

Hello,
Today I'm sharing my experience at the Sky Hub Lounge located in Terminal 2 of Incheon International Airport.

I arrived at Incheon the night before my early-morning KE787 flight to Fukuoka, cleared immigration into the airside area, and spent the night at the SkyHub Lounge.
Having used the lounge in the middle of the night made it a pretty memorable experience.

### Location

![Transfer Lounge 4F signage inside Incheon Airport Terminal 2](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-01.webp)
The SkyHub Lounge is located on the 4th floor of the Terminal 2 departure area.
After clearing immigration into the airside zone, follow the Transfer Lounge signs and take the escalator up.
There are a total of two SkyHub Lounges operating at Incheon International Airport.

![Transfer Lounge A Twosome Place and SKY HUB LOUNGE signage](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-02.webp)

A Twosome Place café is located right next to the lounge entrance.

### Entrance

![SkyHub Lounge entrance corridor with neon sign](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-03.webp)

It was quiet late at night, but the green neon sign at the entrance was quite striking.
The lounge I visited was the western SkyHub Lounge in Terminal 2.
For reference, Terminal 2 has two SkyHub Lounges — east and west. At the time of my visit, the eastern one was closed for renovation.

### Interior

![SkyHub Lounge interior with Live Zone and Plant Forward sections](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-04.webp)

The interior has a clean, warm wood-tone design.
It's divided into themed zones including **Live Zone** and **Plant Forward**, and the two-level layout makes the space feel quite spacious.
Arriving late at night meant almost no one else was around — I essentially had the whole lounge to myself.

### Buffet & Bean Bar

![SkyHub Lounge Bean Bar and buffet food counter](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-05.webp)

The food area is set up buffet-style with a variety of options, and there's a **Bean Bar** coffee counter toward the back.
Considering it was the middle of the night, the selection was reasonably well-stocked — though being late, the food was admittedly on the lighter side.

### Late-Night Meal

![SkyHub Lounge late-night meal with fried rice, pasta, salad, and Shin Ramyun](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-06.webp)

I grabbed a late dinner well past midnight — fried rice, pasta, salad, and a cup of Shin Ramyun.
Having gone all day without a proper meal, that cup of instant noodles in an airport lounge hit differently.

Honestly, just the fact that they offer this much at this hour was more than satisfying enough.

### Hours & Facilities

![SkyHub Lounge app info showing ICN Terminal 2, 4.5 stars, 24-hour operation](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-07.webp)

The SkyHub Lounge operates **24 hours**, but note that **entry is restricted daily from 21:30 to 22:00**.
If you arrive during that window, you'll need to wait about 30 minutes outside.
It holds a 4.5-star rating from 1,816 reviews on LoungeBuddy.

![SkyHub Lounge facilities: Wi-Fi, no smoking, digital card, AC, alcohol, soft drinks, flight monitor](/assets/images/airline-review/icn-skyhub/icn-skyhub-08.webp)

Key facilities:

- **Wi-Fi** available
- **Alcohol & soft drinks** complimentary
- **Air conditioning**
- **Flight information monitors**
- Non-smoking throughout
- **Massage chairs** (I slept in one of these 😴)

### Wrap-Up

I came to Incheon the night before my KE787 Fukuoka flight, had a meal at the SkyHub Lounge, and dozed off in a massage chair.
If you ever need to spend the night at the airport before an early-morning departure, the SkyHub Lounge is a genuinely solid option.

Access is available through a variety of passes including Korean Air mileage cards, Priority Pass, and Lounge Key — worth checking which one you have.
