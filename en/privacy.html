Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Brothrone Travel ("this blog") values the privacy of its visitors. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

This blog does not directly collect personally identifiable information. However, the following data may be automatically collected through third-party services:

  • Visit statistics (page views, session duration, referral sources, etc.)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, screen resolution, etc.)
  • Approximate location (country and city level only)

This information is collected via Google Analytics and Google AdSense solely for the purpose of improving the blog and delivering ads.

2. Cookies

This blog uses cookies provided by Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These cookies help improve user experience and enable personalized advertising.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some functionality may be limited as a result.

3. Third-Party Services

This blog uses the following third-party services. You can review how each service handles data via their respective privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics — Traffic analysis and user behavior statistics.
    Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdSense — Ad delivery.
    Google Privacy Policy →

4. Advertising

This blog displays ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests.

You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the contact page or visit brothrone.org/contact.
